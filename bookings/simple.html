Frequently Asked Questions

How quickly will you call me back?
We typically call back within 2 hours during business hours (9AM-6PM). For requests submitted outside business hours, we'll call you first thing the next business day.
Is there any commitment with this call?
No, there's absolutely no commitment. This is just a consultation to understand your needs and provide initial recommendations.
What information do I need to have ready?
Just a general idea of your event type, approximate date, and number of attendees. Don't worry if you're not sure - we'll guide you through the process.
Do you handle all types of corporate events?
Yes, we specialize in conferences, team building, product launches, training sessions, award ceremonies, and more. We'll discuss your specific needs during our call.
What if I don't know my budget yet?
No problem! We'll provide options at different price points to help you make an informed decision based on your requirements.
Can I email instead of calling?
Of course! If you prefer email, just mention it in your message and we'll follow up via email instead of calling.
