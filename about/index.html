Our Journey

Building Kenya's Event Excellence Since 2019

Scenes & Sets Events was founded with a clear vision: to elevate corporate event standards in Kenya. We saw an opportunity to bring strategic thinking and professional execution to the events industry, transforming how Kenyan businesses approach their corporate gatherings.

Starting from Nairobi, we've grown by focusing on what matters most: understanding our clients' unique needs and delivering events that drive real business results. Our commitment to quality and attention to detail has made us the go-to partner for corporations across Kenya.

While we're proud of our Kenyan roots, our approach combines local expertise with international standards. We believe that every corporate event should be more than just a meeting—it should be a strategic tool for business growth and relationship building.
